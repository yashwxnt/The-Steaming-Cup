“Arguably the best views in Hyderabad. ”

‘Walk into our Beer Garden,’ said the brewer to the barfly. A glasshouse, alfresco and deck nestled between lush foliage and greens, The Hoppery’s split-level space has arguably the best views in Hyderabad.

The Olive Group’s first micro brewery is a pleasant, contemporary all-day space. Its cold, crisp beers pair best with fresh air, notes of songbirds, aromas of flowers in season and wet earth during the day, and then come together with the music as the space turns into a lively night scene.

Feel free to take a stroll between rounds to discover your favourite spot on the property for a peaceful moment, and a gorgeous lake-view. Find your mellow!
